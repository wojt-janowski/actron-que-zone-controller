Wall Controller

Touch panel · Panlee WT32S3-86S (ESP32-S3) · v…

This board has no auto-reset over its USB-serial writer, so you must put it in download mode by hand before clicking Install: hold the pin labelled EN (the silkscreen is swapped — it's really GPIO0) to GND, tap the red RESET button, then release. The panel screen goes black in download mode — that's expected, not a fault. Once it's in download mode, click Install. If the browser can't sync, the CLI path (esptool --before no-reset --after no-reset) is the reliable fallback.
